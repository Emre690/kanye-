Spetsnaz

Information Billboard

Spetsnaz members are the most trained and elite operatives within the TSU, they're fighting on strategic level of combat and handling the most hard tasks within the world. Their equipment consists of: AK47-M, handcuffs, clearance card 3, and some perks.

Spetsgruppas

Spetsgruppa Smerch - largest and most populated Spetsgruppa, Smersh houses the least skilful combatants within Spetsnaz-nonetheless, Smerch operatives are still deemed outstandingly brutal and iron-willed warriors.

Spetsgruppa Vympel - specialised in flanking and reconnaissance, Spetsgruppa Vympel Operatives are known for sneaking up on enemies or taking down targets from long distances. Vympel ranks second in terms of combat skills, taking pride in owning exceptionally deadly sharpshooters.

Spetsgruppa Alfa - is home to the most ruthless and dreadful Operatives within the Soviet Union. Alfa Operatives excel on everything, remaining unsurpassed on the battlefield. Being an Operative for Spetsgruppa Alfa is undoubtedly the most prestigious accolade that the Union has to offer, and all Operatives should train hard to achieve it.

Their duties are to execute especially hard and demanding operations, and eliminating all potential threats who enters the Motherland. They are also fight in the frontlines alongside the Red Army.

Perks

"Vampirism" - Vampirism allows you to regenerate 15hp after every kill, vampirisim lasts infidinately.

"Sleight of hand" - When using this perk, you will get +30% dmg boost, +15% fire rate and +30% quick reload for 10 seconds.

"Camouflage" - When using this perk, you will go invisible for 7 seconds.

"Enhanced Vision" - When using this perk, you will be able to see allies and enemies through all surfaces for 10 seconds.
